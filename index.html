About Us

We are Al-Aqsa company

Al-Aqsa Investment and Agricultural Development Company was established in 2010 by Engineer Shehata Saeed. It is one of the leading companies in the field of land reclamation, agriculture, and irrigation networks, providing the best plant care solutions from seed planting to harvest. In a short period, the company has significantly contributed to the development of the agricultural sector, overcoming challenges and obstacles to achieve the best results by maximizing production per unit area. We continuously strive to keep up with technological advancements by providing the best organic and chemical fertilizers before cultivation, as well as all types of seedlings and greenhouses, ensuring the highest quality agricultural products nationwide. This has enabled us to export to all Arab countries while meeting all required specifications.

Our Services

Solar Energy

Solar energy provides a sustainable and cost-effective solution for agricultural irrigation, reducing reliance on fossil fuels. It ensures a consistent water supply while promoting eco-friendly and efficient farming practices.

Green houses

Greenhouses protect crops from weather fluctuations and ensure consistent production year-round. They create ideal growing conditions, and this sustainable method enhances agricultural efficiency and product quality.

agricultural seedlings

Agricultural seedlings are the foundation of successful farming, ensuring healthy and fast-growing crops. Using high-quality seedlings enhances productivity, improves resistance to diseases, and maximizes yield.

Irrigation networks

Pivot irrigation networks are an efficient and modern solution for large-scale farming, ensuring uniform water distribution. They help conserve water, reduce labor costs, and improve crop yields through precise irrigation control.

Desert land reclamation

Desert land reclamation transforms arid areas into fertile farmland, boosting agricultural production. Through soil improvement and modern irrigation, this reclamation enhances food security and supports economic growth..

Drilling wells

Drilling wells provides a reliable source of water for agriculture, especially in arid regions. It ensures sustainable irrigation and improves water access for agricultural communities. Proper well management also helps conserve groundwater.

Our Goals

Sustainable Agricultural Development

We strive to keep pace with agricultural advancements and provide our customers with the highest quality agricultural products at competitive prices. Our goal is to enhance the productivity of Egyptian crops and support their competitiveness in global markets. Additionally, we focus on developing scientific solutions to improve irrigation systems, especially in newly cultivated lands, to maximize water resource efficiency. We also aim to achieve sustainable agricultural development by adopting the latest technologies that increase productivity while preserving the environment. Supporting farmers is a key priority, and we provide training and advisory programs to enhance their skills and help them implement modern farming techniques. Moreover, we emphasize agricultural manufacturing to add value to crops and boost the export potential of Egyptian agricultural products. By building strong partnerships with local and international institutions, we contribute to the growth of the agricultural sector and the achievement of food security.

frequently asked queries

What services does the company offer in agricultural investment and development?

Our company provides comprehensive services, including high-quality agricultural seedlings, the design and installation of modern irrigation networks, solar-powered irrigation systems, and well drilling to ensure reliable water sources for farms.

Do you provide consultations on selecting the best irrigation systems for farms?

Yes, we offer specialized technical consultations to help clients choose and design the most efficient irrigation systems, whether drip irrigation or pivot irrigation, ensuring water conservation and increased productivity.

What are the benefits of using solar energy for irrigation systems?

Solar-powered irrigation systems reduce operational costs, minimize reliance on traditional energy sources, and ensure continuous operation in an eco-friendly and energy-efficient manner.

Do you offer well drilling services, and what factors determine the drilling location?

Yes, we provide well drilling services using advanced equipment. The drilling location is determined based on detailed geological studies to ensure access to sustainable and high-quality groundwater sources for agricultural activities.
