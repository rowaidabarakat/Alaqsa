About Us

We are Al-Aqsa company

Al-Aqsa Investment and Agricultural Development Company was established in 2010 by Engineer Shehata Saeed. It is one of the leading companies in the field of land reclamation, agriculture, and irrigation networks, providing the best plant care solutions from seed planting to harvest. In a short period, the company has significantly contributed to the development of the agricultural sector, overcoming challenges and obstacles to achieve the best results by maximizing production per unit area.

We continuously strive to keep up with technological advancements by providing the best organic and chemical fertilizers before cultivation, as well as all types of seedlings and greenhouses, ensuring the highest quality agricultural products nationwide. This has enabled us to export to all Arab countries while meeting all required specifications. We also specialize in sustainable agricultural solutions, including solar-powered irrigation systems, well drilling, and desert reclamation projects. Our commitment to innovation has allowed us to transform barren lands into fertile agricultural areas, supporting food security and economic growth. With a dedicated team of experts, we provide farmers with comprehensive guidance, from selecting the right crops to implementing efficient irrigation and greenhouse technologies. Our goal is to enhance productivity while preserving natural resources, ensuring long-term agricultural success. At Al-Aqsa, we prioritize environmental responsibility and modern farming techniques, continuously expanding our services to meet the evolving needs of the agricultural sector. Through strong partnerships and cutting-edge solutions, we remain committed to advancing agricultural development across the region.

We are also committed to expanding the use of renewable energy in agriculture, with a special focus on solar-powered irrigation systems. By harnessing solar energy, we help farmers reduce operational costs, ensure a sustainable water supply, and promote eco-friendly farming practices. This innovation is particularly beneficial for desert reclamation projects, where access to traditional power sources is limited. In addition to irrigation solutions, we provide high-quality agricultural seedlings suited for various climates and soil conditions. Our nursery produces a wide range of fruit trees, vegetables, and ornamental plants, ensuring optimal growth and high productivity. We also offer customized greenhouse solutions, designed to maximize crop yield while protecting plants from harsh weather conditions and pests. Moreover, our well-drilling services support farmers in securing reliable water sources, a crucial factor in successful land cultivation. Using advanced drilling techniques, we identify and access deep groundwater reserves, ensuring sustainable irrigation for long-term agricultural success. At Al-Aqsa, we continue to innovate and adapt to the latest advancements in agricultural technology. Through strategic partnerships, research, and hands-on expertise, we remain dedicated to empowering farmers, increasing food production, and contributing to the overall development of the agricultural sector in Egypt and beyond.
